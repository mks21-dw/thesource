;Using NetLogo syntax, create a procedure borderedRectangle [x y w h c] that forms a rectangle that satisfies the
;following conditions:
;- the top-left corner of the rectangle has coordinates (x, y),
;- the width (horizontal) and height (vertical) of the rectangle is w and h, respectively,
;- the color of the interior of the rectangle is c, where c represents a "true" NetLogo color value, and
;- the rectangle is surrounded by a black border of width 1 (to make the border visible, make the world white).
;Note: It is permissible to use one or more helper functions to make your program more readable. You may want to 
;create a helper function that returns a Boolean value; look up the "report" procedure to do this.
;Skill: 5, 6, 19, and 20
;Possible solution:

to-report isBetween [a x b]
  ifelse ((a <= x) and (x <= b))
  [report true]
  [report false]
end

to unborderedRectangle [x y w h c]
  set pcolor white
  if ((isBetween x pxcor (x + w)) and (isBetween (y - h) pycor y))
  [set pcolor c]
end

to createBorder [x y w h c]
  if ((isBetween (x - 1) pxcor (x + w + 1)) and (isBetween (y - h - 1) pycor (y + 1))) and 
  (not (((isBetween x pxcor (x + w)) and (isBetween (y - h) pycor y))))
  [set pcolor black]
end

to borderedRectangle [x y w h c]
  (unborderedRectangle x y w h c)
  (createBorder x y w h c)
end]]>
